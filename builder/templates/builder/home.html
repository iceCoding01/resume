FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with ResumeBuilder Pro?

Getting started is easy! Simply create a free account, choose a template that fits your career goals, and start adding your information. Our step-by-step process will guide you through creating a professional resume in minutes.

Is my resume ATS-friendly?

Yes! All our templates are designed to be ATS-friendly. Our system also provides suggestions for keywords and content that will help your resume pass through applicant tracking systems used by most employers today.

Can I export my resume in different formats?

Absolutely! You can export your resume in PDF, DOCX, and HTML formats. This gives you flexibility when applying to different positions that might require specific file types.

How can I track my resume's performance?

Our platform provides detailed analytics for each resume you create. You can see how many times your resume has been viewed, downloaded, and more. This helps you understand which versions of your resume are performing best.
